Description
A large, fat woman with a shock of red hair and bright amber eyes. Black and white kimono, worn soft at the shoulders and hem. Lamellar leather pauldrons. A big yellow gourd on a braided cord tied loosely at the wrist and thrown over one shoulder. Built like a sumo wrestler, she pummels foes into submission at close range using a combination of palm thrusts, checks, and grapples. Boisterous, talkative, immediately interested in everyone she meets. Laughs loudly and often. Always asks if you are hungry.
