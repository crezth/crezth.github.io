# Magnetite & The Economy of Hingsajagra

> **Sources:** Core pp. 48–52

## What Is Magnetite? *(Core p. 48)*

The calcified form of Sidhi, pooled around strong sites of spiritual energy. Called **Spirit Stones** by Shen, **energy crystals** or **aetherites** by surrounding cultures.

**1 MAG = 1 USD** (approximate real-world equivalent)

## Physical Properties *(Core pp. 48–49)*

- Manifests as **shards, rods, and staves** of varying grades
- Uniform size: ~2 fingers wide, 1 middle finger long, cut diagonally
- Black when inert; **blaze scarlet** when holding large amounts of Sidhi
- Smallest piece holds at least 1 Sidhi
- Lasts 108 billion years before melting back into Sidhi

## Use-Value vs Exchange-Value *(Core p. 51)*

- **Use-value:** Its Sidhi — capacity for Cultivation. The refinement of mag increases its value due to labor.
- **Exchange-value:** Its role as universal currency in the Post-Calamity world order.
- **The exchange value of mag is NOT equal to its innate Sidhi value.**

### Labor Theory of Magnetite Value
- 1 hour of labor = 1mag
- 1mag = 1 hour of Sidhi (life-force)
- Complex labor (Magick, Ritual, Science, Tech) = 2mag (requires greater skills)
- **Constant Capital** (machinery, materials) + **Variable Capital** (wages) = **Average Profit**

## Wages *(Core p. 52)*

Wages vary by nation. Key exchange rates:
- 1 Ressen-Nalenji Magnetite = 60 Selorongian Magnetite [Sel-Mag]
- Shennin Magnetite, Amatsunese Magnetite [Ama-Mag], Charnel Isles Magnetite [CI-Mag], Isanese Magnetite [Isa-Mag] are all lower than RN-Mag
- Amatsunese economy is nearly as strong as Ressen-Nalenji

## Forms of Magnetite (Currency) *(Core p. 50)*

- **Magnetite chips** — holed and strung in strings. 1mag each, grouped in 10-chip strings.
- **Written credit** — Banks hold magnetite values. Modern international banks arose from **Great Sect Exchange Houses**.
- **Mag-seals** — verification notes for accessing financial accounts
- **Mag-Shrines** — Electrospiritual ATM-like machines powered by electricity and talismans

## The Dual Nature of Magnetite *(Core p. 49)*

Post-Calamity, magnetite primarily serves as **universal currency equivalent** (since the ruled class can't cultivate). Its secondary role as **direct means of cultivation** is banned/demonized.

### The Dark Truth
The Martial Underworld persists because the **Heavenly Ruling Class** waits for cultivators strong enough to reach them — so they can siphon their Sidhi for their own Limit Breaks. Magnetite Veins are finite; Capital's drive to expand is infinite.

## Magnetite Veins *(Core p. 49)*

The greatest Magnetite mines, existing in:
- The Heavenshards
- Jaxiya
- Jeng
- The Charnel Isles

These are the **calcified arteries and lifeblood of the primordial god** that became the islands. They are colonial mines — the most lucrative sources of capital, requiring the most labor.

## Commodity Fetishism *(Core p. 51)*

Magnetite contains real Sidhi but cannot command people, summon flames, or unlock cultivation on its own. Its power comes from its **exchange-value**, not spiritual energy. A sect immortal can charge 10× for a "Heaven-Slaying Spear" that can't actually do what they claim — commodity fetishism in action.
