# Core Mechanics

## Dice

Only uses d6s. Need 3-6 per player.

## Checks

When an action is risky or the outcome is in doubt, make a **Check**:

1. **Abstract** — Intercessor sets the position and the World's **Demand** (target number)
2. **Negation** — Wanderer argues for **Adjustments** based on traits, items, skills, context
3. **Concrete** — Intercessor accepts/rejects adjustments, then the Wanderer rolls **2d6**

### Results
- **Roll ≥ Demand** → Pass. You do what you want.
- **Roll < Demand** → Fail. The World chooses what happens:
  - Sometimes you get what you want but twisted
  - Sometimes you don't get it but something interesting happens
  - Rarely, the World is cruel and bad things happen

### Demand Tiers
| Difficulty | Demand |
|---|---|
| Default | 10 |
| Challenging | 12 |
| Difficult | 14 |
| Heroic | 16 |
| Godly | 18 |
| Impossible | 20 |

Once declared, Demand rarely changes.

### Adjustments [Adj]
Modifiers added to the Check after rolling:
- Helpful tools, good position, assistance: **+1/+2**
- Lack of time, bad position, injury: **-1/-2**

## Health

**Health = 12 + Cultivation Level + higher of Strength or Psyche**

- Falling to 0 Health = instant death
- At half Health or lower = **Bloody** (triggers certain abilities/effects)

## Attacks

**2d6 + Weapon Attribute** vs target's **Parry** or **Dodge**

- On pass: deal damage, subtract target Armor. Double-6s: Critical Damage (+3 dmg)
- On fail: you whiff

## Damage Types
| Type | Effect |
|---|---|
| Cutting | Slashing and slicing |
| Bashing | Ignores up to 1 Armor |
| Piercing | Ignores up to 2 Armor |
| Fire | Ignores all Armor, inflicts Burning |
| Ice | Ignores all Armor, inflicts Cold |
| Volt | Ignores all Armor, inflicts Electrocuted |
| Ultima | Ignores all Armor |
| Psychic | Ignores all Armor |

## Defenses (3 types)
- **Parry** = 6 + higher of Strength or Skill. Melee only.
- **Dodge** = 6 + higher of Mind or Wits. Against all attacks.
- **Resolve** = 6 + higher of Magnetism or Psyche. Mental/magick resistance.

## Conditions
Persistent states that must be resolved through fiction:

- **Burning** — Light Fire damage per tick
- **Bleeding** — Minor Ultima damage on any movement. Must be staunched.
- **Cold** — Cannot move. Movements slowed.
- **Dazed** — Halved damage
- **Encumbered** — Cannot run/sprint/jump. Mobility = 1.
- **Electrocuted** — Cannot act for a tick. Extra damage on hit.
- **Hesitant** — Cannot Attack or do other Hostile Actions
- **Hobbled** — Halved Mobility
- **Poisoned** — Minor Piercing damage every tick start
- **Knockdown** — Ears to floor. Mobility = 1. Vulnerable.
- **Restrained** — Held down, cannot move
- **Stunned** — Cannot act except desperate defenses
- **Staggered** — Blown open. Extra damage from all sources.

## Rest

Full day's rest (12-24 hours) including 2 meals:
- Heal all Health and Strain
- Change memorized Magicks
- Learn new Technicks/Magicks from Manuals, Masters, etc.
