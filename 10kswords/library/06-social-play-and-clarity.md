# Social Play & Clarity

> **Sources:** Core pp. 42–47

## Social Play
*Core p. 42*

Most social situations are resolved through fiction and proper interaction. The core of Social Play is two mechanics: **Leverage** and **Disposition**.

Every Being in the world has a **Culture**, **Truth**, and **Alignment** — appealing to these can itself be Leverage.

### Which Attribute To Roll?
- **Magnetism** — Appealing to emotions, reading emotions
- **Strength** — Threatening, intimidating, sizing them up
- **Magnetism** — Persuading through sheer charisma
- **Wits** — Spinning a grand tale, performing to stir emotions
- **Mind** — Convincing through irresistible rhetoric
- **Psyche** — Attempting to persuade a Spirit

**Without proper Leverage, persuasion suffers -6 Adjustment.**

## Leverage
*Core pp. 42–43*

Something that gives you a way to influence someone more easily. Can be:
- A secret, a want, a vice, a love, a shame, a need
- A pride, a hatred, a duty, a privilege, a lie
- An object, an item, a power

### Leverage Quality → Adjustment
- **No Leverage** — No Adj (default)
- **Good Leverage** (deep secret, a power they want, a duty they're deathbound to keep) — **+2**
- **Exceptionally good Leverage** (love of their life, a secret/shame that would ruin them, a hatred that transcends philosophy) — **+4**

### Getting Leverage
- **Observation** — Watch them for a few minutes
- **Rumors** — Look for information around town
- **Investigation** — Look for evidence while they're away
- **Interaction** — Just talk to them, get to know them
- **Violence** — Threaten with real harm (risky — failing has harsh consequences)

### Written Influence
Influencing through messages or writing works the same way, but ensure the target can read.

## Disposition
*Core p. 44*

Every World Being has a starting **Disposition** — their tendency and reaction to interacting with you.

### The Disposition Roll (2d6)

| Roll | Disposition | Persuasion Adj |
|---|---|---|
| 2- | **Violent** | -12 |
| 3-5 | **Hostile** | -8 |
| 6-8 | **Neutral** | -4 |
| 9-11 | **Friendly** | 0 |
| 12+ | **Enthusiastic** | +2 |

The Intercessor only rolls this if Disposition is unknown. If the fiction has already established how they feel, just set it.

### Factors Affecting Starting Disposition
- Law-abiding citizen + you're dressed like a Law Knight: +1
- Trouble-maker reputation precedes you: -1
- Very friendly/empathetic appearance: +2

### Improving Disposition
Rolling to improve Disposition uses the same mechanics as persuasion. It can be the way to someone's heart.

## Clarity
*Core p. 46*

**The accruing of knowledge and understanding about the world.** A torch lit upon the darkness of ignorance. Clarity measures two things:
1. One's **Direct Realization of Reality**
2. Time spent learning **knowledge** and **lores** of the Present reality

**Maximum Clarity: 12**

## Clarity Stages (CS)
*Core p. 46*

| CS | Clarity | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| ✦ | 4+ | See Spirits in their Subtle Bodies. Most Spirits/Deities have two bodies (Gross and Subtle). |
| ✦✦ | 6+ | In relevant situations, Intercessor gives a physical omen (chill, distant bells, crow cawing 3×). May ask one Yes/No question truthfully. |
| ✦✦✦ | 8+ | People see you more clearly — as someone of great cultivation, leaving the state of the common folk. |
| ✦✦✦✦ | 10+ | Get physical reactions to lies, illusions, or Occult Lore (nausea, ear-ringing, mantra chanting). |
| ✦✦✦✦✦ | 11+ | Attunement to the strange and arcane enhanced. See past illusions of deities, immortals, and devils. |
| ✦✦✦✦✦✦ | 12 | At CL 12: **Extinguish Your Fire** — attain First Stage Enlightenment, become an **Enlightened One**. Remove yourself from the World. If filled with Compassion, become a **Gleaming Saint** (rebirth vessel, starts at CL 3). |

## Spending Clarity
*Core p. 47*

You spend Clarity to know The World — facts, lore, and truths.

| Cost | Lore Level | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **General Lore** | Public knowledge, common facts |
| 2 | **Specialized Lore** | Deep/specialized knowledge, esoteric connections |
| 3 | **Occult Lore** | The most occult of lores, hidden connections between Awakening and Violence |

## Lores
*Core p. 47*

Lores learned from spending Clarity can be written on your Character Sheet. They can be:
- Tapped at any time for relevant actions
- Used as Leverage in Social Play and Social Violence
- Grant a bonus equal to the Clarity spent to buy them

## Earning Clarity
*Core p. 47*

Gain 1 Clarity when you:
- Learn something new
- Experience something new
- Touch/learn/read/see something you're not supposed to

**Intercessors: Do not give out Clarity willy-nilly. It is not XP. It is a currency found in the dark.**
