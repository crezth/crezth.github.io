# Truths & Alignments

> **Sources:** Comp pp. 22–37

## Truths *(Comp pp. 22–34)*

The battlefield of the mind. Paradigms for viewing reality — not just religion but how we perceive reality itself. Called "Truths" instead of "religions" to place ancient faiths on equal footing with modern theory. Roll d66.

### Philosophical Frameworks
| Roll | Truth | Core Belief |
|---|---|---|
| 11-13 | **The Material** | Nothing beyond what we can see. All consciousness arises from material conditions. Every being is a star. |
| 14-16 | **The Ideal** | Fundamental reality is consciousness/spirit/mind. All reality comes from Ideas beyond the mind. |

### Eolomism & Yenjanism (God-faiths)
| Roll | Truth | Core Belief |
|---|---|---|
| 21 | **Eolomism** | Militant faith of Eolo, God of Uncreated Light. Goal: the great Rejoining. Spread Holy Tenets to teach God-In-Nature. |
| 22-24 | **Yenjanism** | Faith of High Prophet Yenja. God as the Allcaring (Love). Yenja achieved apotheosis on the Stake. Return to God through Love of All. |
| 25 | **Imperial Yenjanism** | Ancient orthodoxy. Raten as divine emanation. Led by the Highest Star. Emulate Yenja's suffering for divine union. |
| 26 | **Dissenterism** | Protestant Yenjanism. Connect God to common man without priests. State faith of Soreh. Puritanical/liberal values. Synced with Machine God → Wealth Gospel. |
| 31 | **Folk Yenjanism** | Yenjanism mixed with spirits, amulets, talismans, Samadhi, sacred geometry. Heretical per Imperial Church. Local beliefs strongest. |
