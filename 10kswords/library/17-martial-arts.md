# Esoteric Martial Arts (Technicks)

> **Sources:** Comp pp. 66–97

Martial Arts are used to end Violence. Esoteric Martial Arts grant special **Technicks** that wield Sidhi. Must be learned from a master or manuscript — cannot learn from nothing. Each Technick requires knowing the previous one in sequence. Each Technick is weapon-specific.

**Violent Enlightenment** — The ultimate Technick of each Art. Requires Overwhelming Strain + Samadhi + knowing all other Technicks first.

*(Comp pp. 66–68)*

## Empty Hand Arts
*Comp pp. 68–72*

### Vajra Boxing
*Punching, parrying, deflecting, counters, locking, low kicks, multiple strikes.*

1. **Vajra Guard** — Minor Strain. Auto-parry next melee attack with perfect adamant timing.
2. **Devastating Red Hand** — Minor Strain on-hit. Explosive secondary impact + Blazing.
3. **Gentle Lion Reversal** — Minor Strain on parry. Immediate counter-attack exploiting imbalance.
4. **Raging River Fist** — Minor Strain on-hit. Immediate second damage dealing (kinetic backlash follow-up).
5. **Divine Guardian Locking Technique** — Heavy Strain on-hit. Complex locking maneuver: Severe Bashing + Stun.
6. **Destroyer Trident Combination** — Samadhi + Heavy Strain + 2 Actions. Four Burning Attacks with movement between, ending with Divine Fire vajra crash.
- **VE: Ten Thousand Enlightened Hands** — Ungodly Ultima damage to all hostiles in 100hx radius.

### Diamond Body
*Elbows, knees, feet, hands as hardpoints. Grappling with iron limbs.*

1. **Vajra Strike** — Minor Strain on-hit. Attack gains Blazing, Frost, or Fulgur (choose 1).
2. **Demon Carapace Body** — Minor Strain. Attacks Strengthened for 1 minute.
3. **Iron Shirt Attainment** — Moderate Strain. All Non-Psychic Damage Resist 1 for 1 min (stackable to 3).
4. **Vajra Sutra Bone Contemplation** — Moderate Strain. Store all damage received in Inner Vajra Sutra for 1 Round, then unleash as Volt damage on-hit.
5. **Vajra Monster Unchaining** — Samadhi + Heavy Strain. +4 Str, -2 Skill/Wits/Mind for 1 Round. Become vajra monster.
6. **Heaven-Shattering Adamant Strike** — Samadhi + Heavy Strain. On-hit: Extreme Bashing + push Str×5 hexes.
- **VE: Devour The Face Of God** — Immune to all non-Psychic damage for 1 Incense.

## Sword Arts
*Comp pp. 72–76*

### Adamantine Sword
*The peerless, ultimate cut. Created by sword-saints for the Revolution.*

1. **Adamantine Lightning Strikes** — Minor Strain. Two immediate Attacks.
2. **Glare-Lightning Mirror** — Moderate Strain. Perfectly parry next attack (even ranged/magick).
3. **Steel Chrysanthemum Blooming** — Minor Strain. Attack all in Burst 2hx (omnidirectional slashes).
4. **Flying Self Carving God Flash** — Heavy Strain. Throw weapon 10hx, teleport to it, Strengthened Attack.
5. **Samadhi Sword Glare-Lightning** — Samadhi + Moderate Strain. 6hx Line of Twice-Strengthened Volt Damage.
6. **Steel Flower Breaks Heaven** — Samadhi + Heavy Strain. Dash 3×Mobility hx ignoring terrain, Attack all passed through with Strengthened Damage.
- **VE: Ego-Cutting Sword God** — 5 flying swords orbit you (60hx range, +2 Accuracy, Strengthened), ride a sword to fly.

### Sword Dialectic
*The balanced sword art taught to the masses during the First World Revolution.*

1. **Negation Cut** — Minor Strain. After hitting same target twice, leave a Negation. Detonate within 1 min to repeat last damage.
2. **Even-Handed Regard For Uncertain Ideas** — Moderate Strain. +1 Parry + crit on 11+ (single) or no dual-wield penalty (paired) for 5 min.
3. **Critique of the Weapon** — Minor Strain. Free Interrupt per tick to counter-attack threats to self/allies.
4. **Weapon of Critique** — Minor Strain. On-hit: extra damage + target takes extra damage from everything until next tick.
5. **Movement of the Immense Majority** — Samadhi + Moderate Strain. Three potential Attacks, choose which becomes real (Strengthened + extra + psychic on crit).
6. **Abolish the Present State of Things** — Samadhi + Heavy Strain. On being hit: halve damage, split into two afterimages, choose which shatters.
- **VE: Relentless Critique of All That Exists** — Split into three combatants for 5 min. Shattered copies deal 2d6 psychic burst.

## Saber Arts
*Comp pp. 77–78*

*Assassination art of the Violet Lotus Sect. Shadow, stealth, teleportation.*

1. **Death Lotus Enfolding** — Minor Strain. Stealth: 3-in-6 miss chance, teleport 2hx on miss. Attacking from stealth = surprise.
2. **Death Lotus Unfurling Slash** — Minor Strain. Brutal heavy slashing + Cold.
3. **Thorny Gloam Pirouette** — Moderate Strain. +2 Dodge for tick, counter + teleport 2hx on dodge.
4. **Indigo Umbra Chassé** — Samadhi + Moderate Strain. Teleport Mobility×2, stealth + obscured terrain 2hx.
5. **Dusk and Noon Armament** — Minor Strain. Create Cold Brutal Holy Saber for 30 min.
6. **Seven Shadow Striking Sutra** — Samadhi + Moderate Strain. +2 combo Attack, teleport 1hx after each Attack.
- **VE: Gloamblossom Assassination** — Disappear into Deep Gloam Lotus. Next Action: instantly kill any Bloody target within 60hx (or divine frost damage if their CL is higher).

2. **Candle Shadow Flutter Tear** — Moderate Strain. Teleport 5hx, undodgeable azure fire attack.

4. **Specter Consuming Edge** — Minor Strain. Ultima damage attack, heal half the damage dealt.
5. **Ghost-Expelling Rite Blade** — Moderate Strain. On-hit: eject target's soul 8hx behind, Knockdown.
6. **Charnel Self-Cutting** — Minor Strain. Create shadow copies (1+Realm max). Redirect attacks to shadows, use shadows as action origin points.
- **VE: The Maiden's Atomic Blade** — Thrice-Strengthened Ultima damage ×6 (uninterruptible, unblockable). If target dies: erased from Akasha.

## Cleaver Arts
*Comp pp. 81–84*

*Perfect cleaving, giant weapon mastery, fury channeling.*

1. **Grinning Asura Attitude** — Minor Strain. 100hx burst Agitation: all equal/lower CL targets must target you or suffer -6.
2. **Six Demon Cutting** — Minor Strain. Thrice-Strengthened single vertical arc attack.

4. **Devil King Throne** — Samadhi + Heavy Strain. Immovable stance (Mobility 2). Next Six Demon Cutting becomes Twelve-fold Devouring Satan Cut: unblockable, Strengthened×4, destroys armor, Stun + Knockdown.
5. **Hatred Condensation Seal** — Accumulate up to 6 Hatred (1 per damage taken, Minor Strain). Release all as extra damage dice on-hit.
6. **Spiteful Disembowelment of Seraphim** — Samadhi + Moderate Strain. Launch target 10hx up, pursue airborne, target Stunned in sky, take first Action next tick uninterrupted.

## Bludgeon Arts
*Comp pp. 84–89*

### Black Vajra Pestle
*Blacksmith/crafter art. Hammer and pestle. Revolutionary creation.*

1. **Hate-Shattering Bliss Mace** — Minor Strain + 2 Actions. Unblockable attack that destroys target's weapon (or frays Artifacts).
2. **Iron Tiger Club** — Minor Strain + 2 Actions. Unblockable, destroys weapon instead of damage (breaks Artifacts).
3. **Metom's Chakra Rod** — Minor Strain. Summon pureblack artifact bludgeon (30hx throw, ignores gravity). Lasts 30 min. Max 1+Realm.
4. **Adamantine Noose** — Moderate Strain. 100hx indestructible noose. Restrain or slide target 20hx.
5. **Metomian Puppetry Formation** — Samadhi + Heavy Strain + 2 Actions. Create Battle-Puppet combatant (DR = your Health). Max 1+Realm.
6. **Vajra** — Samadhi + Heavy Strain. Summon vajra weapon. Unblockable, Divine Ultima damage. Implodes after attack.
- **VE: Means of Production Nirmanakaya** — Grow 4 magitech arms (Chakra Rod + Vajra). +4 Skill/Wits/Mind. Repair/destroy armor in 6hx radius.

### Sapphire Star Dialectics
*Healing martial art. Compassion Sidhi. Parasol-focused.*

1. **Scything Pure Wind** — Minor Strain. 10hx curve heals ¼ max Health.
2. **Ten Thousand Spirit Flame Acupoint** — Minor Strain. Touch target: +2 Mobility, +1 Skill, +1 Wits for 3 ticks.
3. **Sapphire Star Barrage** — Moderate Strain. 2hx burst: heal allies (Psyche), attack enemies' Resolve (medium fire).
4. **Gentle Petal Glide** — Minor Strain. Glide 1hx above ground, normal Mobility. 1 min.
5. **Sapphire Canopy Deflection** — Samadhi + Minor Strain. Perfect parry, redirect attack to someone else.
6. **Elixir Halo** — Samadhi. Healing shard orbits you or ally. Heals 2 Health after any damage taken.
- **VE: Sapphire Mandala Blossom** — Heal all chosen in 50hx of all damage, +1 all Attributes for 1 min. For 1 min: free Reverse Compassion Beam (massive psychic ultima) or Pure Compassion Beam (6d6 heal) each tick.
