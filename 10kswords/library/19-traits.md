## Dynamic Trait Creation *(Comp p. 56)*

Traits can emerge from play. Guidelines:
- Must come from fiction (not from nothing)
- Numerical bonuses/penalties: max +1/-1
- Skill bonuses need a corresponding penalty
- Avoid doubling — don't stack similar effects (leads to min-maxing)
