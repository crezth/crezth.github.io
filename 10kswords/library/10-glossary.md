# Glossary of Key Terms

> **Sources:** Core pp. 4–5; Comp throughout

## Metaphysical / Cosmological *(Core pp. 4–5)*

**Akasha** — The inframaterial, underlying, non-local field of active information and eternal memory. A field of Information from which observable reality arises. Ultimately void.

**Dialectics** — The philosophical way of understanding reality through constant change and interpenetrating interconnectedness. Three laws: Law of Contradiction (unity of opposites), Law of Negation of the Negation, and Law of Transformation of Quantity into Quality. Also refers to a Way, Path, Dao, or Dharma.

**Hawa** — The vital life breath within sentient beings, carrying Sidhi through channels, meridians, and spinning chakra-centers.

**Karma** — The weight of a being's Sidhi. Not the soul, but the fluctuating mindstream. Intentional actions imprint upon atoms.

**Sidhi** — Active energy flowing from the Akasha. Quintessence arising from dialectic. Non-local and inframaterial energy of the Trichiliocosm. Allows paranormal effects.

**Supreme Dialectic** — The perfect unity of all opposition. The ultimate force. The realization of Reality as Violence. Ultraviolence. The secret name of God.

**Trichiliocosm** — The full cosmos / universe.

## Cultivation *(Core pp. 4–5, 56–62)*

**Cultivation** — Refining one's Cultivation Womb to grow in material and inframaterial strength, leading toward Enlightenment. Requires devouring items with strong Sidhi indexes.

**Cultivation Dialectic** — The path and system of one's Cultivation. Determines shape, directory, and sources. Five types: Revolution, Internal Elixir, Luminous Mind, Communion, and one more.

**Cultivation Womb** — The organ within the body (pineal gland to liver) connecting to the Akasha. The nascent immortal/Omniscient resting within you. Fed accumulated Sidhi from Spiritual Treasures, Magnetite, etc.

**Enlightenment** — Directly realizing the true nature of reality. Ineffable and beyond words but material all the same. Destroys all dualisms, makes you Deathless, an expression of Supreme Dialectic.

**Enlightened One** — A being that has attained Enlightenment and become a Great Adept. Deathless. Upon death, can choose to stay conscious in formless unity with chaos.

**Limit Break** — The process of advancing to the next Cultivation Stage. Requires seclusion + training OR performing a Great Work for a community, followed by a Tribulation.

**Samadhi** — State of unbreakable meditative concentration. Action and consciousness merge. Prerequisites: deep control of mind or Sidhi. Heals 1 Sidhi on entry, grants +1 to Dialectic-defined Attribute.

**Strain** — The stress of wielding Sidhi. Fills the Sidhi track. Minor=1, Moderate=2, Heavy=4, Overwhelming=6. When full, excess Strain deals 3 Ultima damage each.

**Tribulation** — A vision quest/trance at the end of a Limit Break where you fight your Cultivation Womb manifest. Defeating it lets you consume and absorb its power.

## Beings & Entities *(Core pp. 4–5, 93–95)*

**Deity** — A human subspecies from primordial tribes that established kingdoms in orbital heavens. Two forgotten tribes: Chthonic (subterranean) and Astral (heavens). Born with access to Cultivation Methods.

**Spirit** — All paranormal beings. Ogres, asuras, goblins, pixies, angels, seraphim, demons, devils. Made of Sidhi upon patterns of Akasha.

**Wizard** — Accomplished great adepts that can wield huge amounts of Sidhi and master Mystic Arts. From Old Isanese *veizza* (Accomplished).

**World Being (WB)** — Any character the Intercessor controls. NPCs, monsters, spirits. Expressions of the World.

**Wandering Dead** — Common Post-Calamity monster. Corpses reanimated by lingering hateful ghosts. Deathform trait.

## Material / Economic *(Core pp. 20–23, 48–52)*

**Magnetite** — The calcified form of Sidhi. Spirit Stones. Universal currency in Post-Calamity. 1mag ≈ 1 USD. Grades: Low (1), High (100), Divine (1,000), Supreme (10,000).

**Devil Engine** — Machines that turn karma/spirits into energy. Giant spirit house with sacrificial furnace. Fueled by Devil Juice. Invented by Vajra Monk Rakan Jesung under Machine War God Ziben.

**Devil Juice** — Living fuel for Devil Engines. Cans or jugs of mixed spiritual material.

**Mag-Shrine** — Electrospiritual ATM-like machine for magnetite transactions.

**Black Nectar** — The Petroleum Lord of Akashic Capital. The Oil God. The ultimate revenant of every dead being.

## Esoteric Arts *(Core pp. 5, 58; Comp pp. 66–122)*

**Esoteric Arts** — Secret techniques for wielding Sidhi to produce paranormal effects. Comes from Mystic Arts or Martial Arts.

**Technicks** — Physical/martial techniques enhanced by Sidhi.

**Magicks** — Sorcerous/mystical techniques powered by Sidhi.

**Divine Empowerment: Bolster** — Universal Art. Minor Strain. +2 Strength Checks, +2 melee damage + knockback.

**Divine Empowerment: Lightness** — Universal Art. Minor Strain. Weight manipulation, wall-jumping, floating movement, reduced fall damage.

## World / Political *(Core pp. 8, 14–15, 19)*

**Five Triumphant Immortals** — The five who ended communism and rule the new heavens: Mentra, Galdasigwa, Saint Ashtasi, Lord Atthur, Reyayu Kodaku.

**Five-Armed Demiurge Array** — The technique enacted by the Five Triumphant Immortals that extinguishes Sidhi and seals Cultivation from the Masses. Must be overcome via the Antinomian Rite.

**Antinomian Rite** — A rupturous, transgressive act that momentarily breaks one out of the Five-Armed Demiurge Array, igniting the Cultivation Womb.

**Realms Belligerent / The Underworld** — The martial art underground functioning outside the neoliberal World Order. Where martial artists cultivate technicks, clash with iron wizards, and ideologies come to a head.

**10,000 Dragons** — Those who see Communism and Revolution with contempt, as "mountaineers," "foresters," banditry, and piracy.

**Amalgamated States of Ressen-Nalenji** — The last great Empire. Ultimate Colonialist State.

**Magofascist Empire of Amatsu** — One of the four surviving international powers.

**Semi-Socialist Celestial Shennin Society** — One of the four surviving powers, infiltrated by enemies.

**109th Hell: Selorong, Center of the World** — One of the four surviving powers.

## Game Terminology *(Core pp. 12–13)*

**Intercessor** — The GM equivalent. A spiritworker and shaman who speaks to The World.

**Wanderer** — A PC. Someone surviving the Wheel, from the Masses, who has begun to Cultivate.

**Telegraph** — The Intercessor's description of the World's state. What it does to you.

**Action** — The Wanderer's response. Their Struggle.

**Consequence** — How the World changes in response. Becomes the new Telegraph.

**Check** — 2d6 vs Demand when an action is risky. Abstract → Negation → Concrete.

**Demand** — The target number for a Check. Default 10.

**Adjustment** — Modifiers to a Check based on context, tools, conditions.

**Ultraviolence** — Pure Violence that does not turn the Wheel of Law-Making and Law-Preserving. Destructive, annihilative, atoning. The only real recourse.
