# The World of Hingsajagra — Setting & History

> **Sources:** Core pp. 14–25

## The World Is...
*Core pp. 14–15*

- **Like Ours** — Materialism as baseline. Four Fundamental Forces are the Four Violences. Aesthetic of 1900s Asia at the liminal edge of progress and tradition.
- **Tropical Gothic Ruin** — Hot, humid, diseased. Gothic horror meets tropical reality. Colonial violence, melancholy, ruins of ancient deities.
- **Old And Weary** — A thousand empires have risen and fallen. Millennia of history. Modern time is republics and nation-states under the Amalgamated States of Ressen-Nalenji.
- **Oppressed by Immortality** — Mass Cultivation made impossible by the Ruling Class. Immortal lords cultivated through oppression. Demon Path Cultivation.
- **Gripped By Capital** — All patriarchy, hegemony, oppression has evolved to serve **Black Nectar, the Petroleum Lord of Akashic Capital**. Nations must participate in the Market.
- **Teeming With Fickle Gods** — More Spirits than humans, more Gods than Spirits. All violent, all fickle.
- **Eschaton** — Post-Calamity. The **Ultima Bomb** destroyed the Lotus Throne. The only international powers: **Magofascist Empire of Amatsu**, **Ressen-Nalenji**, **Semi-Socialist Celestial Shennin Society**, and the **109th Hell: Selorong**.
- **Ultraviolent** — Churches of cannibals, ghosts haunting the living, swordmasters scourged by sorcerers. The **Realms Belligerent** — the martial underworld.

## Geography — The Utter Islands
*Core pp. 24–25*

The final island continent in a flooded world. Built upon the corpse of the Lotus God, consumed by the Earth Mother. Key regions visible on the map:
- **Nilatpa** — Large central landmass
- **Jaxiya** — Western region
- **Jeng** — Where the Lotus Throne was destroyed (hallowed island)
- **Kij Dakmala** — The Nail of the World
- **Wadzra** — Eastern region
- **Pemi** — Southern region
- **Charnel Isles** — Between major landmasses
- **The Heavenshards** — Southern fragments
- **Refuge of the Gnostics** — Northwestern
- **The End of the World** — Far south

## The Nine Eras — The March of History
*Core pp. 16–19*

Current year: **11,664** of the Lotus Calendar (243rd Cycle). Parallel to our 20th century but industry is driven by magick and war.

### 1 — Peregrination Era (0–2000)
*Core p. 16*
Hunting-gathering, primitive communism. Deities and Astral Bureaucracy interfaced directly. First Revolution: Agricultural.

### 2 — Burgeoning Era (2000–8000)
*Core p. 16*
Agricultural Revolution. Chiefdoms, warbands, kingdoms. Ended with The Ultimate Cleave — apocalyptic war of the Utter Islands against the Heavens, led by the Imperial King In Red. Time of Heroes.

### 3 — Esoterica Era (9000s–10,000)
*Core p. 17*
Ritual Revolution. Mystics and Wizards ascend. Iron-fist rule of mago-philosophers. Ended when warrior-kings overthrew wizard-priests.

### 4 — Ancient Era (10,000–10,500)
*Core p. 17*
Warrior Revolution. Sublation of magical into priestly/knightly classes. Black Meteor Cataclysm shattered the Utter Islands further.

### 5 — Classical Era (10,500–11,300)
*Core p. 17*
Aristocratic Revolution. Rise of classical cultures. Old Razru Empires, Lotus States, Shen civilization. Ended with victory over Devil King Roghan.

### 6 — Rebirth Era (11,300–11,500)
*Core p. 18*
Industrial Revolution. Smith-Kings and Merchant Princes. Invention of the **Devil Engine**. Textile factories, weapon factories, machine tools.

### 7 — Modern Era (11,500–11,600)
*Core p. 18*
Bourgeois Revolution. World Colonialism and Capitalism. Merchant princes seized means of production. Even deities subsumed into the labor force. Ended with the First World Revolution led by **Kafeng Masangwa** and the Knights.

### 8 — Revolution Era (11,600–11,640)
*Core p. 18*
Proletarian Revolution (attempted). Factory proletariat rose but was beaten into "Peace" by the International Bourgeoisie. **Second World Revolution failed.** Capitalism won the second turn of the Wheel of Violence.

### 9 — Post-Calamity Era (11,640–11,664...)
*Core p. 19*
The End of the World. International bourgeoisie (Central Yavinian Guild of Ressen-Nalenji) penetrates the world. **The Five Triumphant Immortals** enacted the **Five-Armed Demiurge Array** — sealing Cultivation from the Masses. Communism and Revolution now seen as banditry. The **Underworld / Realms Belligerent** rises from old martial traditions.

## The Five Triumphant Immortals
*Core pp. 8, 19*

The rulers of the new heavens, who ended communism:

1. **Mentra**, the Moondancer, Immortal of Sorcery — sits on Lunar Heaven, granted Enlightenment for all (ironically)
2. **Galdasigwa**, the Immortal of War — took the largest piece of Heaven as a Blade, shields world from demon armies on Outer Heaven
3. **Saint Ashtasi**, the Immortal of Swords — sits on Broken Heaven, the greatest cutter
4. **Lord Atthur**, the God of Fortune, Immortal of Gold — sits on Capital Heaven, Soreh of the Amalgamated States of Ressen-Nalenji, the High Lord of International Free Trade, God of Neocolony
5. **Reyayu Kodaku**, the Immortal of Immortals — controls the Socialist Party of the Celestial Shennin Society from the shadows

## The Devil Engine
*Core pp. 20–23*

The greatest invention of humanity and the beginning of its consummation. Machines that turn karma into energy.

- Resembles a giant spirit house with a closed-in sacrificial furnace
- Burns devils, spirits, weaponsouls, ghosts, people, and mantra-mandalas
- Invented by **Vajra Monk Rakan Jesung** (working-class machinist), who contacted **Machine War God Ziben** (a skillful incarnation of **Enlightened One Yosotuku**)
- Fueled by living **Devil Juice** — cans or jugs of mixed spiritual fuel
- **Magnetite Engines** are the ethical alternative (used in socialist Hokou and Selorong)

## The Destruction of the Lotus Throne
*Core p. 23*

- In 11,640, the **United Union of Peace** (international bourgeoisie) created the **Ultima Bomb** via the **NFHR** (Nilatpan Facility for Hildemej Research)
- A **Mahabrahma** — war magotech powered by the blood and bones of a Mahabrahma
- Destroyed the Lotus Throne atop **Kij Dakmala** on the island of **Jeng**
- The Lotus Throne was sat upon by deity **Sunggats-i Malayozo**, placed by Kafeng Masangwa
- Its destruction severed teleportation between Heavens/Hells and Hingsajagra
- Shattered reality across the Utter Islands, reshaping nations
