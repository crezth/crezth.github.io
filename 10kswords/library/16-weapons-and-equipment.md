# Weapons, Equipment & Items

> **Sources:** Comp pp. 40–49

## Weapon Basics
*Comp p. 42*

All beings have 2 Weapon Slots. Empty hand counts as a weapon. Weapons have a **pattern** (base type) and **properties** (modifiers).

## Key Weapon Properties
*Comp pp. 43–44*

- **Arcane** — Channels magick. Accuracy Mind/Psyche. Ultima Damage. Range Psyche+Mind hx.
- **Artifact** — Ancient relic. +[Realm] Attack Check, +[Realm] Damage Tier.
- **Brutal** — +1 Damage Tier, -2 Accuracy
- **Colossal** — 2 Actions, +2 Damage Tiers, Doublehand, 3 Carry, Stun, must re-ready after attack
- **Concealable** — Can be folded/hidden
- **Disarming** — +2 disarm attempts
- **Doublehand** — Both weapon slots, -1 Skill
- **Ferocious** — Crits on natural 11+
- **Focus** — Replaces mudras
- **Great** — 2 Actions, +1 Damage Tier, Doublehand, 2 Carry
- **Holy** — Extra damage + fire vs Spirits
- **Instrument** — Makes music, can use +Magnetism or +Psyche
- **Reach** — +1 Range
- **Ritual** — +1 ritual checks
- **Shield** — +1 Parry, can Parry ranged attacks
- **Thrown X** — Can be thrown X hexes
- **Worn** — Part of clothing, cannot be disarmed
- **Blazing/Frost/Fulgur** — Deals fire/ice/volt damage

## Weapon Upgrading
*Comp p. 46*

| Form | Requirement | Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| Refined (+1) | 5 Siderite Shards + blacksmith | +1 Damage Tier |
| Divine (+2) | 3 Siderite Fragments + blacksmith | +2 Damage Tiers |
| Immortal (+3) | 1 Siderite Core + grandmaster blacksmith | +3 Damage Tiers |

**Siderite** falls from the heavens, imbued with Astral Sidhi.
- Shard: 50mag
- Fragment: 500mag
- Core: 5,000mag

## Weapon Modification
*Comp p. 47*

Pay 750mag for a blacksmith to add a new property:
- Refined: up to 2 properties (1 week each)
- Divine: up to 3 properties (3 weeks each)
- Immortal: up to 4 properties (1 month each)

Self-modification possible with crafting Trait — requires Skill Checks.

## Attire Slots
*Comp pp. 48–49*

Four slots: **Head**, **Chest**, **Arms**, **Legs**. Each provides stat bonuses/penalties. Armor comes from attire.

### Examples (1 Armor pieces, most expensive)
- **Full Helmet** — 1 Armor, -1 Wits, -1 Psyche. 3,500mag.
- **Lamellar Suit** — 1 Armor, -1 Skill, -1 Psyche. 5,000mag.
- **Plated Gauntlets** — 1 Armor, can't do Magicks, -1 Skill. 2,500mag.
- **Plated Legs** — 1 Armor, -1 Skill. 3,500mag.
