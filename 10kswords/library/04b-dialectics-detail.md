# Cultivation Dialectics

> **Sources:** Comp pp. 59–67

The Compendium's full treatment of each Dialectic: its philosophy, Cultivation method, Limit Break Rite, Samadhi trigger, Perversions, and Attainment list.

---

## Right Hand vs. Left Hand Dialectics
*Comp p. 59*

Cultivation Dialectics are the paths a Cultivator intentionally chooses to cultivate toward Enlightenment. They recognize the **Supreme Dialectic** (also called the **Eternal Dialectic**): the ultimate reality of change, impermanence, and struggle.

Dialectics split into two camps:

- **Right Hand Dialectics** — systems used by Cultivators who work *within* the Heavenly Bourgeoisie's World Order. These are system-compliant, respectable, socially sanctioned. There is no need to enumerate these for the likes of you, Wanderer.
- **Left Hand Dialectics** — unknown or forbidden by the Bourgeoisie. Far more numerous than Right Hand paths. They survive because eradicating them would cost more than tolerating them. The path of the Wanderer.

Wanderers are by definition outsiders to the bourgeoisie system of Right Hand Dialectics, and so always follow the path of one of the Left Hand Dialectics of Revolution, Internal Elixir, Luminous Mind, and Communion

## Revolution Dialectics
*Comp pp. 60–61 — The Dialectic of Mystic Materialism*

Cultivating by violence, suffering, talisman Sidhi absorption, tendon-knitting massage, occult substance imbibement. The old traditions of the Soul Heralds — revolutionary spirit-healers of indigenous communities, largely genocided under colonization. Adopted by much of the Selorongian Commune's Communist revolutionaries. Considered the culmination of centuries of Masangwanist theory, applied by Dattreya Wairini.

### Struggle Cultivation
The Revolution Dialectician gains **100mag per enemy** fought in clear, Liquid Steel Ultraviolence.

### Limit Break Rite: Moon-Bleeding Rite
Performed under a full moon (when Sidhi is most volatile and the Moon Immortal Mentra — one of the Five-Armed Demiurge Array — is weakest). Collectively ingest an **Elixir of Revolution** (aborted fetus, semen, menstruation blood, and material of past revolutionaries), cut each other, then bathe in a natural hot spring untouched by Capital. Afterward, your Cultivation Womb takes the shape of the **Keris of the Masses** — a keris piercing upward.

### Revolutionary Samadhi
While in Samadhi, you don the **Ragged Garb of the Revolutionary**:
- +1 Strength
- You and all allies within 5hx gain **All Damage Resist 1**

**Enter Samadhi when you…**
- Score a Critical Hit
- Save an ally from a fatal blow
- Fall to ¼ max Health for the first time in an hour
- Bask in revolutionary fervor in the midst of a Mass Movement/Strike/Protest and pass a Demand (11 + your Stage) Mind check

### Perversions — "Devil Goblin / Angel Guerrilla" kind
*Roll or pick on Limit Break:*
1. A swoosh, much quieter voice echoes your speech in indecipherable language
2. Horns, snake eyes, devil wings, or longer fangs
3. Skin starts to glow, as if burning from within
4. A subtle halo or celestial ribbon-sash follows your movements
5. Sage tattoos cleave across your body
6. Flowers bloom or wither where you walk, if on soil

### Attainments
- **Rushing Ragasa** — While in Samadhi, your Mobility is doubled and you and all allies' Attack Checks gain +1.
- **Revolutionary Tenacity** — +1 Strength permanently.
- **Punk Tactics** — When attacking someone outnumbered, deal extra damage.
- **Immanent Dialectics** — +1 Mind permanently.
- **Move As One** — When you enter violence, you and all allies enter Samadhi for that tick immediately.
- **Revolutionary Alacrity** — While in Samadhi, +1 Action for the tick you entered Samadhi; otherwise all your Checks gain +4.
- **Massive Sidhi Reserve** — Can transfer Strain to a willing ally in sight, or take an ally's Strain upon yourself.
- **Thousand Hands Reaching To The Workers** — Gain two more hands (four total). Gain 2 more Weapon Slots.
- **Body Talisman Refinement** — Resist 1 vs all damage except Ultima and Psychic. In Samadhi, immune to Cutting and Piercing.
- **Supreme** — While amongst your allied crowd, allies cannot die (only be knocked out). Effect ends when you're knocked out.

## Internal Elixir Dialectic
*Comp pp. 62–63 — The Dialectic of the Self-Cauldron*

Cultivation by perfection: meditation, body movements, potions, manual study, martial and mystic arts. The goal is to perfect your Cultivation Womb and eventually ingest it to become an Immortal. "Internal Elixir" because the final Elixir to consume is yourself.

### Elixir Cultivation
Gain **double magnetite** from drinking elixirs while refining Sidhi.

### Limit Break Rite: Self-Cauldron Rite
Sit for a week in a cauldron of soul wine deep enough to submerge you, brewed with: **Heart Talisman, Spirit Ginger, 6 Spirit Stones, 6 Aethicites, 6 precious gems** (any). Set the cauldron alight and infuse the flame with your Sidhi. After the week, drink the contents and undergo Tribulation. Your Cultivation Womb takes the shape of the **Eight Trigrams** — the geometrical perfection of Universe and Man.

### Internal Elixir Samadhi
While in Samadhi, the **Perfected Furnace**:
- +1 Psyche
- All Technicks and Magicks cost one less Strain tier (Heavy → Moderate, etc.)

**Enter Samadhi when you…**
- Score a Critical Hit
- Last at least 6 ticks against overwhelming odds (Samadhi triggers on the 7th tick)
- Defeat an opponent
- Spend a tick meditating and pass a Demand (12 + your Stage) Mind check

### Perversions — "Uncanny Perfection" kind
1. Pupils become diamond-shaped
2. A phantom face beside your first face
3. Grow to a freakishly tall height
4. Hands longer than your torso, reaching below your knees
5. Perfectly even teeth, exactly 40
6. Eyes become an unsettling blue-green

### Attainments
- **Diamond Ability** — +1 Wits permanently.
- **Deception-Piercing Gaze** — See clearly in low light (pitch-black still weakens you).
- **Adamantine Soul** — +1 Psyche permanently.
- **Ultimate Cauldron** — +1 Skill permanently.
- **True Concentration** — Can suffer Heavy Strain to enter Samadhi for the rest of the tick.
- **Exude Fire** — In Samadhi, immune to fire. Attacks deal fire damage and can ignite.
- **Diamond Form** — +1 natural Armor.
- **Marriage of Heaven and Earth** — All Magick checks and rolls roll 3d6, keep highest 2.
- **Weapon Dialectics** — Choose a weapon pattern; all your Attacks with it gain +1.
- **Demonic Path Practice** — When you kill someone, absorb their Sidhi as mag equal to 5× their Cultivation Level.

## Luminous Mind Dialectic
*Comp pp. 64–65 — The Dialectic of the Non-Self*

Cultivating the realization of Non-Self — the union of Wisdom and Compassion, Father and Mother of all Enlightened Ones. One of the oldest Dialectics, predating the first Enlightened One, developed by the Pyre-Sages and Sky Mendicants of Razru. Obfuscated under Capitalism by wellness gurus who reduced it to mental health marketing. Arises from the Enlightened One's liberatory logic: the cultivation of Anticraving and Unignorance, untethering from the Suffering caused by craving Pleasure, Existence, and Non-Existence.

### Meditation Cultivation
Gain **500mag** by meditating and achieving Samadhi in front of a ritual furnace pot. **Once a month.**

### Limit Break Rite: Hermitage Rite
Retreat to a place of silence for a week. No food, no drink. **Lose 1 Health per day.** Each day, recite the mantra and hold perfect Tranquility until you attain one-pointed concentration. The goal is Supersight into the **Three Marks of Existence**: Non-Self, Impermanence, Suffering. At the end, you must beat your Tribulation with whatever Health remains. Your Cultivation Womb takes the shape of the **Void Mirror** — a circle empty of content, representing the primordial union of Emptiness and Mind.

### Luminous Mind Trigger (Samadhi)
**Ultra Void Insight:**
- +1 Mind
- All Defenses +2

**Enter Samadhi when you…**
- Score a Critical Hit
- In violence, don't suffer damage for a tick
- Stay perfectly still for 30 minutes
- Meditate for a tick and pass a Demand (12 + your Stage) Mind check

### Perversions — "Radiant Mirror" kind
1. Eyes become pure white; hands become longer than your torso
2. Hair permanently turns bright white and bioluminescent
3. Become unable to lie — if you do, your eyes bleed liquid Light
4. Skin becomes so pale people can see slightly through you
5. Flowers, crystals, or tiny bells spontaneously appear in your hair and never fall off
6. No pulse anymore; breath is cold

### Attainments
- **Droplet** — +1 Psyche permanently.
- **Psychic Mirror** — Psychic Resist [Realm].
- **Cut Through Ego** — +1 Skill permanently.
- **Grand Compassion** — Outside Violence, allies' Mind/Wits/Psyche checks +1. In Violence, allies in 3hx burst heal 1 every time they start their Action.
- **Incantor** — After casting a Magick, heal 1 Health for all allies *or* deal minor psychic damage to all hostiles in a 3hx burst.
- **Immanent Luminosity** — Emit a 10hx-burst faint glow. All your Ultima damage deals +1 vs Demonic beings.
- **Void Soul** — In Samadhi, non-Ultima/non-Psychic attacks have 3-in-6 chance of missing outright (rolled before Attack Check).
- **Sword of Wisdom** — Empty Hand and Dagger attacks may deal Psychic damage instead.
- **Bell of Compassion** — On a Critical Hit, all allies within 5hx heal 1 Sidhi.
- **Gleaming Saint Application** — In Samadhi, every time you suffer damage, allies in burst 3hx heal 2 Health.

## Communion Dialectic
*Comp pp. 66–67 — The Dialectic of Gods and Kings*

The Cultivation of Reverence. Most Communion followers have a religion or spirituality, but it isn't required — it's just an opening, a chasm to fall into. The Womb is refined through parlaying, contending, and performing **pacts and vows** with Spirits and Deities beyond mortal ken. Practices pass down through priesthoods, cults, shamanisms, mendicants, and monk systems.

Most Communion Cultivators dedicate to a single Divinity — Prima (Yenjanist Goddess), Eolo (God of All-Consuming Light), Kanzen of the Northern Star, Luzbel of the Evening Star, the Blasted Sun, Mirona the Ocean Goddess, and so on. Powerful Spirits count too; common among saint healers and shamans without a single supreme being.

### Worship Cultivation
Gain **100mag per day** for offering to a deity or spirit at a shrine or altar.

### Limit Break Rite: Covenant of the Throne of Fire
Spend a week in the domain of the divinity most powerful to you. Protect what lives there from malevolent manifestations of its karma. Then **be possessed by the divinity for a day**. Afterward, defeat the Divinity-Possessed Cultivation Womb as your Tribulation. **Failure means the Divinity takes your body as a vessel.** Your Cultivation Womb takes the shape of the **Throne** — the vessel for divine will.

### Communion Samadhi
**High Seraph Mantle:**
- +1 Magnetism
- All attacks gain Holy
- Emit light up to 50hx

**Enter Samadhi for a tick when you…**
- Score a Critical Hit
- Once per Violence, pray to a nearby Shrine or Altar during Violence
- Completely submit yourself to the Greater Powers for an hour
- Sink into deep Contemplation or Ritual Trance and pass a Demand (12 + your Stage)

### Perversions — "Anima Stigmata" kind
1. Holes erupt around your hands, feet, and brow
2. Antler horns erupt from your brow
3. Become unable to eat one kind of food — eating it causes extreme nausea, vomiting, 2d6 crushing damage
4. Skin becomes so pale people can see slightly through you
5. A second set of eyes appears occasionally above or below your first
6. Wings grow around your waist or chest, can be folded; feathers grow in random spots

### Attainments
- **Charism** — +1 Magnetism permanently.
- **Saint Soul** — Projected voice commands attention, especially from Spirits.
- **Divinity Manifest** — +1 Strength permanently.
- **This Is My Body** — Allies within 5hx gain Magick and Psychic Resist 2. Hostile Spirits and unwanted Spirits must pass a Mind Check (Demand 10 + your Stage) to enter the burst or target you with possession.
- **Pure Rebuke** — Once per tick, push a Spirit within 2hx of you up to 10hx away.
- **High Seraph** — In Samadhi, body becomes irrevocably angelic: +1 Strength, Magick Resist 2, +1 on grabbing attempts.
- **Inquisition** — Sense lies and broken oaths as a shimmering crimson halo — hidden betrayals, stolen offerings, secret pacts. Requires conscious effort to interpret.
- **Inner Shrine** — No longer need an external shrine to perform rites, offerings, sacrifices. Your bosom is consecrated.
- **Divine Weapon** — Choose any weapon within 24 hours. It becomes Strengthened permanently and can never be destroyed or disarmed from you.
- **Mattatrun's Voice** — In Samadhi, +1 Magnetism; wield your voice as a weapon (**Accuracy Magnetism, Range 3hx burst, Heavy Psychic damage, push 3**).

## Caveats

- The Compendium's Revolution Attainments list duplicates **Rushing Ragasa** (once with the definition, once without). The list above merges this — treating it as a single Attainment.
- Pages 63, 65, and 67 all use a templated Perversions header that mis-labels later Dialectics as "Internal Elixir Perversions." The *kind descriptor* (Uncanny Perfection / Radiant Mirror / Anima Stigmata) and the actual roll table make the correct Dialectic obvious — this is a layout copy-paste oversight.
- Right Hand Dialectics are referenced but not detailed anywhere I can find in the Compendium. Supposedly they are reserved for advanced/GM-facing material.
